URGENT Emergency Release Notification
Native Meet Plugin 2.9.7.7
  • Add compatibility with macOS 10.12 Sierra
    • Deployment Date/Time

    To be deployed on Sep 19th between 7 to 11 PM PST

    What is the issue fixed in this Emergency Release?

    New users joining the Meet with Moxtra Audio or Video or Screen Share are not able to install the plug-in in the new version of Mac OS (10.12) to be released by Apple on Sep 20th

    Impact:
  • Existing Mac users who already have the plug-in installed in their machine will auto upgrade to the new plugin. New users will be installing the plug-in as before (no impact to them). No impact to non Mac users.
  • Existing Mac Desktop Client users of Moxtra application will also be upgraded.
